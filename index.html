About US

Newport Minerals Limited was established in 2020 as a family run business specializing in the manufacturing of ground minerals for industrial and agricultural uses. Over the years NML has grown into a fully-fledged Industry for classified (graded) Calcium Carbonate, Kaolin and Dolomite,ground to varying consistencies and supplied to various manufacturing industries. Newport Minerals Limited is committed to customer satisfaction and has devised a self- certification programme that employs performance measurement. We are constantly achieving 100% quality.
