Welcome to Newport Minerals Limited

Newport Minerals Limited was established in 2020 as a family run business specializing in the manufacturing of ground minerals for industrial and agricultural uses. Over the years NML has grown into a fully-fledged Industry for classified (graded) Calcium Carbonate, Kaolin and Dolomite, ground to varying consistencies and supplied to various manufacturing industries. Newport Minerals Limited is committed to customer satisfaction and has devised a self- certification programme that employs performance measurement. We are constantly achieving 100% quality.

Our process

Newport Minerals Limited employs European technology in manufacturing processes. It is one of the leading mining companies for minerals in East and Central with an installed capacity of 40,000MT, well-equipped machines for manufacturing customised products as per client requirements. We employ lean manufacturing principles which are embedded not just in the operations of the company, but also engrained in the minds of our staff. Our policy of suppling products and services is based on Quality Right First Time (QRFT) and under strict guidelines of Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). We have been setting standards since 2020, by producing a variety of minerals for different applications. We mainly focus on the production of fillers derived from Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC), Kaolin and Dolomite thus providing a wide range of product solutions that contribute to our customers’ competitiveness and productivity across a multitude of industries. Our ultrafine and micro fine fillers are used to manufacture products that serve in the following industries: Construction, Paint, Plastics, Paper Manufacture, Cosmetics, Rubber, Packaging, Food, Agriculture, Livestock, Pharmaceuticals, Ceramics, Terrazzo, Foam, Glass industry and other FMCG industries.

The brand promise

Newport Minerals Limited is committed to excellence in everything we do. We are consistent and transparent in the way we operate our business. More so, we pride ourselves in adapting to our clients’ ever changing needs, therefore stay relevant to them and the constantly changing market. Key measuring principles are QRFT.
